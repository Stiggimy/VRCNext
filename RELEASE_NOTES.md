**2026.16.0**

**Sharable Messenger Content**
With sharable Messenger Content, you can copy an Avatar ID, World ID, Profile ID, and more, then paste it into Messenger. When you send one of these IDs through the VRCN Messenger, your friend will see a preview of the content and can click it to open it directly in VRCNext.

* Added Worlds
* Added Groups
* Added Avatars
* Added Profiles
* Added Events

* Added "Dates" to messages. Before it was only times without the dates when a message was send.

**VR Overlay**
* Added Time/Water/Notf. Dashboard
The Dashboard now includes a new tab that shows a clock, the time you have spent in VRChat during your current session, the previous instance you were in, and a warning if you have not drunk water for at least one hour. You can enable, disable, and configure the water alert. I thought this would be a nice feature since I often forget to drink water in VRChat because time passes very quickly.

* Added new **Clock** tab
* Added **Drink Water** warning
